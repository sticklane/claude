# Normalize `Next stage:` closing lines across the Antigravity port

Of the 14 `.claude/skills/*/SKILL.md` files that carry a `Next stage:`
closing line per CLAUDE.md's authoring conventions, only 3 of their
`antigravity/.agents/skills/*/SKILL.md` mirrors carry the equivalent line —
surfaced independently by both the design (task 02) and distill (task 03)
audits in this spec, which each left the gap alone as likely-systematic
rather than a per-skill incidental drop. Worth a dedicated pass to confirm
whether the omission is a deliberate port convention (Antigravity's
successor-stage discovery works differently, so the line doesn't apply) or
an accumulated gap worth closing skill-by-skill.
